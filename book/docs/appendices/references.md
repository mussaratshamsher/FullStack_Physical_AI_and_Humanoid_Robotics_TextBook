# References

This appendix lists references and further reading materials relevant to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## Academic Papers & Journals

### Foundational Works
- Brooks, R. A. (1991). "Intelligence without representation." *Artificial Intelligence*, 47(1-3), 139-159. This seminal paper argues that intelligent behavior can emerge from simple interactions with the environment without requiring complex symbolic representations.

- Pfeifer, R., & Scheier, C. (1999). "Understanding Intelligence." *MIT Press*. Comprehensive exploration of embodied cognition and its implications for robotics and AI.

- Metta, G., Natale, L., Nori, F., Sandini, G., Vernon, D., Fadiga, L., ... & Orazio, P. D. (2010). "The iCub humanoid robot: An open-systems platform for research in cognitive development." *Neural Networks*, 23(8-9), 1125-1134. Introduction of the iCub robot as a platform for research in embodied cognition.

### Physical AI and Embodied Cognition
- Pfeifer, R., Lungarella, M., & Iida, F. (2007). "Self-organization, embodiment, and biologically inspired robotics." *Science*, 318(5853), 1088-1093. Discusses how embodiment and self-organization can lead to robust behavior in robots.

- Bongard, J. (2013). "Evolutionary robotics." *Communications of the ACM*, 56(8), 74-83. Overview of evolutionary approaches to developing robot controllers and morphology.

- Zhu, Y., Mottaghi, R., Kolve, E., Lim, J. J., Gupta, A., Fei-Fei, L., & Farhadi, A. (2017). "Target-driven visual navigation in indoor scenes using deep reinforcement learning." *IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)*, 3357-3364. Important work on visual navigation for robots using deep RL.

- Sadeghi, F., & Levine, S. (2017). "CAD2RL: Real single-image flight without a single real image." *Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 1991-1998. Demonstrates sim-to-real transfer for robotic learning.

### Humanoid Robotics
- Kajita, S., Kanehiro, F., Kaneko, K., Fujiwara, K., Harada, K., Yokoi, K., & Hirukawa, H. (2003). "Resolved momentum control: Humanoid motion planning based on the linear and angular momenta." *IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*, 1644-1650. Key paper on humanoid motion planning using momentum-based control.

- Pratt, J., King, H., Brown, H., Ko, J., Claman, D., & Holley, J. (2006). "Capture point: A step toward humanoid push recovery." *IEEE-RAS International Conference on Humanoid Robots*, 200-207. Introduces the capture point concept for balance recovery in humanoid robots.

- Hofmann, A., Liu, M., & D'Andrea, R. (2008). "Design, construction and control of a single-motor-driven robot." *IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 3717-3724. Innovative approach to simplifying humanoid robot design.

- Hutter, M., Gehring, C., Jud, D., Lauber, A., Diethelm, R., Bachs, T., ... & Siegwart, R. (2016). "An autonomous humanoid robot for playing ball." *Journal of Field Robotics*, 33(5), 564-577. Example of complex humanoid robot applications.

### Manipulation and Control
- Hogan, N. (1985). "Impedance control: An approach to manipulation." *Journal of Dynamic Systems, Measurement, and Control*, 107(1), 1-24. Classic paper introducing impedance control for robotic manipulation.

- Khatib, O. (1987). "A unified approach for motion and force control of robot manipulators: The operational space formulation." *IEEE Journal on Robotics and Automation*, 3(1), 43-53. Foundational work on operational space control.

- De Luca, A., & Ferrajoli, L. (2009). "A comparison of admittance control techniques for physical human-robot interaction." *European Control Conference*, 2349-2356. Comparison of control strategies for safe human-robot interaction.

- Gienger, M., Groten, R., & Steil, J. J. (2011). "Learning inverse kinematics: Comparative evaluation." *IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks*, 22(12), 2307-2319. Evaluation of different inverse kinematics learning approaches.

## Books & Monographs

- Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (Eds.). (2016). "Springer Handbook of Robotics." Springer. Comprehensive reference covering all aspects of robotics research and development.

- Spong, M. W., Hutchinson, S., & Vidyasagar, M. (2020). "Robot Modeling and Control" (2nd ed.). Wiley. Standard textbook on mathematical modeling and control of robotic systems.

- Murray, R. M., Li, Z., & Sastry, S. S. (1994). "A Mathematical Introduction to Robotic Manipulation." CRC Press. Mathematical foundation for understanding robot mechanics and control.

- Thrun, S., Burgard, W., & Fox, D. (2005). "Probabilistic Robotics." MIT Press. Comprehensive treatment of probabilistic methods in robotics, including localization, mapping, and planning.

- Russell, S., & Norvig, P. (2020). "Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach" (4th ed.). Pearson. Standard AI textbook with significant coverage of agent-based systems relevant to embodied AI.
